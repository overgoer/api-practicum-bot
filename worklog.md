- Безопасность через sandboxing опции (NoNewPrivileges, ProtectSystem и т.д.)

**Пример конфигурации:**
```ini
[Unit]
Description=API Practicum Telegram Bot
After=network.target
Wants=network.target

[Service]
Type=simple
User=claude_agent
WorkingDirectory=/home/claude_agent/projects/api_practicum_bot
ExecStart=/usr/bin/python3 main.py
Restart=always
RestartSec=10
StandardOutput=journal
StandardError=journal

# Защита
NoNewPrivileges=true
ProtectSystem=strict
ProtectHome=true
PrivateTmp=true

[Install]
WantedBy=multi-user.target
```
